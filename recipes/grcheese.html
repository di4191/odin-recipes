Simple Grilled Cheese

It's cheese and bread. There's not much to it.

A favorite of picky children and lazy adults, grilled cheese sandwiches are usually one of the first things that most people learn to make in the kitchen. Pair it with a bowl of tomato soup and you'll have lunch in no time.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Cheese Slices
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add a slice of cheese (or however many you want). Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  3. Recall fond childhood memories of grilled cheese and ponder how something so simple can still be this satisfying.
